Who we are

AstroTech Studio is a team of passionate astronomers, developers, and educators dedicated to making space exploration accessible to everyone. Founded in 2020, we've grown from a small startup to a leading educational technology platform, serving thousands of students and space enthusiasts worldwide. Our multidisciplinary approach combines scientific accuracy with engaging storytelling and cutting-edge web technology
